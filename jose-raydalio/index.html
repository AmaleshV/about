Contradictory way of tackling a Situation

amalesh
Blog
0

It was July 10th, 2016, I was my first hour in Venice around 10:05 pm Giudecca Bella, hearing some loud cheers from a bar out there, decided to check out. It was Euro final, Portugal vs France. It was an instinctive interest that was built towards the sport after watching the passion of everyone especially ‘7’, Which has driven me towards watching the tactical side of football and managers.

Having a strong passion towards how to manage people and football, communal between these, lead me to one of a kind of person called José Mourinho. It was him given me a different view on winning is not the way everyone thinks of how to win but the way you tackle an intuitive situation. To build Strong interpersonal thinking and passion was changed from his time at FC Porto to Manchester United. But this confidence in him and to give it to a team losing 2-0 against Newcastle United.

Coaching is taking a player where they can’t take themselves

                                                                                                                    – José

Given me a different perspective of how psychology could do Impossible things happen, shown me the way you think is what you become and achieve. In brief, Jose is a critic of professionalism, behavior, and work in public which to lead to a gap in progressing this passion towards the people he manages.

Talking about Ray Dalio, came across a TED talk called How to build a company where the best ideas win. This leads to me reading his book called Principles of Ray Dalio. By this time I don’t know how much extent a book can inspire. It was an eye-opener for me to see the things as it is rather not to complicate by thinking more. This is so vague to explain but in reality, most of them don’t follow. Then came across this words of Radical Truth and Radical Transparency, in brief, it’s about being transparently truthful towards the people you manage. This has given me a different perspective on how we can achieve a set of goals collectively by being radically transparent.

The two main people who have influenced me a lot are so contradictory in their principles and philosophy. Jose and Ray Dalio both are right in their own way but is strongly influenced by these two people has made me stronger in making decisions by being self-critical, radically transparent to tackle any situation.
